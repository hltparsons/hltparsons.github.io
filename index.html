V I S I O N

of Learning

My vision for learning is to create an environment in which learners are empowered to develop critical thinking, confidence, and take pride in their learning in a context relevant to their own lives; where effort and risks are acknowledged and appreciated; where students are represented, can lift each other up, and construct knowledge together through collaboration; in which I am a facilitator of growth and positive change.

Goals

  • To develop relationships with students so that I may attend to their social-emotional and academic needs, set high expectations and boundaries, and so that we can learn from one another.
  • To use learning tasks that allow students to apply concepts to their own lives, both by bringing previous knowledge to their learning and applying their knowledge in areas that are relevant to themselves.
  • To encourage and make space for students to collaborate with one another and work independently to further their knowledge and their development of learning skills.
  • To incorporate and honour different cultural ways of knowing in the classroom by decentering Eurocentric cultural values historically viewed as neutral.

Beliefs

  • Personal relationships are integral to student learning.
  • Social-Emotional Learning is relevant in all content areas.
  • Learning does not happen in a vacuum.
  • Teacher and student self-efficacy is instrumental to the learning environment.
  • Project-Based Learning is important for academic, socio-economic, and cultural inclusion.

Values

  • teacher-student relationships

  • collaborative learning

  • learning in context

  • confidence & independence in learning

  • affective content knowledge

  • attention to learning, sensitivity to students, & challenge

  • people and ideas are always developing

  • teacher as facilitators, not authority figures

  • assessment for learning
