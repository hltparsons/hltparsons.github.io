V I S I O N

of Learning

My vision for learning is to create an environment in which learners are empowered to develop critical thinking, confidence, and ownership of their learning; where effort and risks are acknowledged; where students are represented and can lift each other up; in which I am a facilitator of growth and positive change.

Goals

  • To develop relationships with students such that we can learn from one another.
  • To use learning tasks that allow students to apply concepts to their own lives.
  • To encourage and make space for collaborative development of knowledge and skills.
  • To incorporate and honour various cultural ways of knowing
